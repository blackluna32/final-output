SEMINAR ON FORENSIC BALLISTICS

  • Category:Seminar
  • Venue:Balangaw Hall
  • Date: 28 April, 2023
  • facebook Page: https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100082805198617

This program aimed to provide the Criminology Students the necessary knowledge and understanding on Forensic Ballistics in relation to investigation and crime detection that are essential for their future endeavors.
The Guest Speaker of the Program is from the Negros Occidental Provincial Forensic Unit PSSg Arnel L Dela Cruz, Ballistician and PCpl Jolibert A Vingco, Fingerprint Expert who shared their expertise, valuable lesson and give motivation to future law enforcer.
